About Us

Welcome to Luwero Secondary School Alumni (LUSOSA), an organization created to connect graduates of Luwero Secondary School and to promote the school's vision and mission. Our aim is to bring together former students of the school to foster networking, mentorship, and support for the current and future students of the school.

We believe that as alumni, we have a role to play in ensuring that the school continues to thrive and produce successful graduates. Through LUSOSA, we can stay connected, share our experiences and resources, and work together towards a common goal of promoting education and community development in Luwero and beyond.

Join us today and become part of a growing community of Luwero Secondary School alumni dedicated to making a positive impact in the world!
